How The Prob Portfolio Works
01
We only trade when the crowd has conviction.
We skip coin flips. A trade only logs when the market is priced at 65% or higher (we buy YES) or 35% or lower (we buy NO). No maybes, no middle ground.
02
$100 paper trade on every qualifying pick.
We log a $100 position at the current market price. We don't cherry-pick winners after the fact. Every call is logged at the time of the pick, before the outcome is known.
03
P&L closes when the market resolves.
When a market resolves YES or NO (above 95% or below 5%), the trade closes automatically. Wins scale with the odds — a bold call at 70% pays less than a contrarian call at 30%. Losses are capped at $100.
