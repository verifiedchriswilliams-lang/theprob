What This Is
Every day, millions of dollars get wagered on prediction markets. Will there be a recession? Who wins the next election? Does Bitcoin hit $150K before July?
People put real money on these questions. When real money moves, it means something.
The crowd isn't always right. But it's usually faster than the headlines.
